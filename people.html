THE TEAM

Foo Kai Lin -
Photographer
Kai Lin is one of the two Photographers at Dolce Vita Studio. She is also the proud Mumma of Tubby !

Kai Lin has won awards for her animal portraiture and always looks to give back to animals through charities and rescues. Kai Lin grew up in a household full of fur babies and learnt the respect and love our fur family give us from a young age.
Selina Liu -
Photographer
Selina is our other photographer, who works hand in hand with Kai Lin.

When Selina isn’t in the studio, she is usually spending time with her fur babies Comet and Churro, as well as her human family. With Selina as your photographer, you and your fur babies will be in good hands and have a wonderful experience together.
Coslynn Choo -
Animal Specialist
Coslynn is our very own in house Animal Specialist. She has been professionally taking care of animals for 10 years now, and knows by now the different ways to treat and take care of different animals.

Aside from being a cat mom to Meow, Coslynn loves spending her extra time reading and learning more about animal behaviour.
Rachel Ann Mathews -
Design Specialist
Rachel, our in house design consultant, is passionate about producing the best art pieces for you here at Dolce Vita.

Rachel loves the process of working with clients and bringing to life what they envision on their digital platforms, and is always challenging herself to grow her aesthetic eye. In her free time, she enjoys creating funny videos of her favourite pawson - Willow the floof.
