COVID-19, UPDATE

Dolce Vita Studio remains committed to doing our part in protecting all who comes through our doors.

We have examined every touchpoint within our establishments and developed new best-in-class safety and hygiene practices to see us through the long-term.
As an SG Clean Establishment, our hygiene and sanitation measures have been inspected by the appointed assessors and our staff are happy and healthy.
Hand Sanitiser
Hand sanitizing stations are being installed throughout the studio, from Reception to elevator landings, to the waiting rooms and main photography room.
Social Distancing
Signage in studio will remind our guests to maintain social distancing, and each group will have to adhere to the 5 people policy to ensure utmost safety.
Guest Holding Rooms
In the studio we have elevated our rigorous protocols to thoroughly clean all surfaces with hospital-grade disinfectants every 2 hours, and we will be placing disinfecting gel and wipes in the studio for your use.
New Cleaning Technologies
We are rolling out enhanced technologies including electrostatic sprayers to sanitize surfaces throughout the studio. We are also testing ultraviolet light technology for sanitizing devices shared by our guests.
