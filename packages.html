OUR PACKAGES

Beautiful portraiture doesn’t just happen, it takes a lot of time, planning and experience. To make sure that each of our artworks is beautiful, personal and telling your story, we go on a journey with each and every one of our clients.
THE DOLCE VITA EXPERIENCE
1. YOUR PLANNING CONSULTATION

The journey starts with having a conversation over Zoom, about you and your pets in the Planning Consultation, and continues at the Studio Session with Selina, our photographer.
2. YOUR PHOTOGRAPHY SESSION

Next you get to relax in style as you view your pets photo being taken. Selina will help design your artwork, and place your order at the Design and Ordering Consultation after.
3. YOUR DESIGN AND ORDERING

Finally, this is where all the preparation and work comes together; you select the photos you love most, view them in mock-ups of our various wall art designs and collection so you can see what they will look like displayed on your own walls in your home.
