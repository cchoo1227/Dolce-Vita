About our packages

 
What packages do you offer?
We offer two types of packages:
- weekday two-hour studio session
- weekend two-hour studio session
You can choose whether or not to include a wall art in either package but 90% of our customers do and you will not regret it!
What are the prices of each package?
Weekday two-hour studio session - $165
Weekend two-hour studio session - $220
Please note that you will be charged additionally for the wall art.
Which package should I choose?
We suggest you come on a weekday if possible as we are slightly overbooked for the weekends and there might be longer waiting time for you and your fur-babies.
Can I change my photoshoot date after booking?
Yes, you may. If there are valid reasons causing you to be unable to make it for the session booked, we are more than willing to work things out with you. Simply drop us an email so we can arrange another session for you.
